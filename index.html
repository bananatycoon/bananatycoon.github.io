Woah! An actual update? Wait! Don't go into shock just yet. I'm aware that it's been around a year since I've shown this little project any love and that's my bad. If you know me in real life you know I've been doing a bunch of other - equally stupid - stuff. 

So... this looks different. You're right, it does. That's because it is. Upgrade names are the same for the most part, but, other than that and the general concept, pretty much everything has been changed or updated. The largest change is probably the addition of per second gaining and, I have to say, this should have been a day one addition. Another major thingy is just general code cleaning although that's not interesting to anybody but me. There's now a big ol' banana to click which is fun too. Saving and loading works better, there're more animations and graphics. It also works on mobile now!

That's the general gist, I guess. Once you close this window it won't be in your way again thanks to the wonders of   t e c h n o l o g y™. Have fun!
